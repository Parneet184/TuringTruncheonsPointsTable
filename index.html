Evaluation Criteria (100 Points)

  • Consistency (20)
    Regular participation and sustained effort throughout the week.
  • Activity Execution (25)
    Planning and successful completion of meaningful club activities such as workshops, competitions, challenges, quizzes, campaigns, discussions, online technical sessions, or community initiatives.
  • Teamwork (15)
    Collaboration among members, contribution sharing, and collective effort towards club goals.
  • Innovation (15)
    Creativity, originality, and the introduction of fresh ideas or unique approaches.
  • Participation Rate (15)
    Level of member involvement and engagement in the week's activities.
  • LinkedIn Visibility (10)
    Professional promotion of club activities through LinkedIn posts, achievements, and updates.

  • Inter-Club Collaboration – Meaningful collaboration between two or more clubs.
  • Special Achievement – Recognition, milestone, or notable accomplishment during the week.
